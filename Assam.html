Welcome to Assam

About Assam Hotels Things to Do

Assam, known as the "Land of the Red River and Blue Hills," is a state located in the northeastern part of India. It is famous for its rich biodiversity, tea plantations, and vibrant culture. Explore the lush green landscapes, wildlife sanctuaries, and historic monuments that make Assam a unique destination.

Natural Beauty

Assam is blessed with picturesque landscapes, including the Brahmaputra River, tea gardens, and lush green hills. Explore the Kaziranga National Park, home to the endangered one-horned rhinoceros, and the Manas National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Cultural Heritage

Assam boasts a rich cultural heritage with diverse ethnic communities. Experience the traditional Bihu dance, visit ancient temples like Kamakhya Temple and Sivasagar Sivadol, and explore the silk weaving villages to witness the intricate craftsmanship of Assamese textiles.

Tea Plantations

Assam is famous for its tea plantations, producing some of the finest teas in the world. Visit tea estates in places like Jorhat, Dibrugarh, and Tinsukia to learn about the tea-making process and enjoy a refreshing cup of Assam tea.

Major Tourist Attractions

Some of the major tourist attractions in Assam include the Kamakhya Temple, Kaziranga National Park, Majuli Island, Sivasagar historical sites, and the scenic hill station of Haflong.

Things to Do in Assam

  • Take a Jeep Safari in Kaziranga National Park
  • Visit the Kamakhya Temple in Guwahati
  • Explore the Majuli Island - World's Largest River Island
  • Experience the Bihu Festival
  • Take a River Cruise on the Brahmaputra River
