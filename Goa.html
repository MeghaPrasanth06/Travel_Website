Welcome to Goa

About Goa Hotels Things to Do

Goa, is a state on the southwestern coast of India within the Konkan region, geographically separated from the Deccan highlands by the Western Ghats. It is bound by the Indian states of Maharashtra to the north, and Karnataka to the east and south, with the Arabian Sea in the west

Cultural Heritage

The culture of Goa is an amalgamation of Indian and Portuguese culture. The crafts and arts of Goa also reflect Hindu, Muslim and Christian origins.

History

The long Portuguese rule has influenced the culture in more ways than one and many of the Portuguese traditions are reflected in the Goan culture even today. The crafts and arts of Goa also reflect Hindu, Muslim and Christian origins.

Major Tourist Attractions

Some of the major tourist attractions in Goa include the the Sunburn party, Arjuna beach, Chapora Fort ,Baga beach.

Peak Time to Visit

Ideally,the besttime to visit Goa is in November, December, January and February which is the peak season and July, August, September and October which is the moderate season.

Things to Do in Goa

  • Experience the famous Sunburn party
  • Visit Baga Beach
  • Explore the various Forts in Goa
  • Take a nature Trail at Chorao Island
