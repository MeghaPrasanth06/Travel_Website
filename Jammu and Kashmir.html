About Jammu and Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir, often referred to as 'Paradise on Earth', is a region of great natural beauty and cultural diversity. From the towering peaks of the Himalayas to the serene lakes and valleys, Jammu and Kashmir offer a mesmerizing experience for travelers.

Cultural Diversity

Jammu and Kashmir is known for its rich cultural heritage, influenced by Hindu, Buddhist, and Islamic traditions. Explore the vibrant festivals, local cuisine, and age-old traditions that reflect the diversity of the region.

Major Tourist Attractions

Some of the major tourist attractions in Jammu and Kashmir include the Dal Lake in Srinagar, the Mughal Gardens, Gulmarg for skiing, the ancient temples of Jammu, and the Amarnath Yatra.

Peak Time to Visit

The best time to visit Jammu and Kashmir is during the months of April to October, when the weather is pleasant and conducive for outdoor activities. However, each season offers its own unique experiences, so it depends on your preferences.

Things to Do in Jammu and Kashmir

  • Take a Shikara ride on Dal Lake
  • Visit the Mughal Gardens in Srinagar
  • Explore the ancient temples of Jammu
  • Experience the thrill of skiing in Gulmarg
  • Embark on the Amarnath Yatra
