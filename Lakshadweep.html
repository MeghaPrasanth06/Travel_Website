About Lakshadweep

Lakshadweep, meaning "a hundred thousand islands" in Sanskrit, is a tropical archipelago off the southwestern coast of India. Known for its pristine beaches, coral reefs, and tranquil lagoons, Lakshadweep is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Island Hopping

Explore the different islands of Lakshadweep, each offering its unique charm and attractions. From the bustling life of Kavaratti to the serene beaches of Agatti, there's something for everyone.

Water Sports

Indulge in a variety of water sports activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, kayaking, and windsurfing amidst the crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine life of Lakshadweep. Kayaking through tranquil lagoons, windsurfing across the azure waves, and parasailing high above the islands provide thrilling experiences for adventure seekers. Jet skiing offers an adrenaline-fueled rush, while banana boat rides promise fun for groups.

Local Cuisine

Experience the rich and flavorful cuisine of Lakshadweep, influenced by Kerala and Arabic culinary traditions. Don't miss trying the seafood delicacies and refreshing coconut-based dishes. Tuna is a popular fish used in traditional recipes like 'Tuna Thoran,' a dry dish cooked with grated coconut and spices. 'Kerala Parotta' and 'Pathiri,' flatbreads made from rice flour, accompany many meals, along with flavorful rice dishes like 'Biriyani' and 'Ghee Rice.

Activities in Lakshadweep

  • Snorkeling in the coral reefs
  • Scuba diving to explore marine life
  • Kayaking in the lagoons
  • Windsurfing on the azure waters
  • Island hopping to discover hidden gems
  • Relaxing on pristine beaches
  • Exploring local culture and traditions
